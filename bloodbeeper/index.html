👋 Welcome to BloodBeeper

Your app is pre-loaded with 3 months of sample data so you can explore all the features right away — the chart, trends, and analysis report.

When you're ready to track your real BP readings, go to Your Data → Erase All below and start fresh.

☁️ Data syncs across all your devices automatically. Use Save to Spreadsheet to export a copy for your doctor.
